Graphite

Project to re-design the box of a popular board game. I chose Battleship.

Laser-cut the ship and text shapes, then printed them by hand with a very thick ink to give a gritty texture. Scanned the prints and arranged and colored in Photoshop. Printed on a plotter and glued to the original box.

I think the gritty texture is a nice touch for a game about sending your opponent to a watery grave.
