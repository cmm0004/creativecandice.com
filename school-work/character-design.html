Character Design

Project to re-design a character from a children's book. I chose the protagonist from "Put Me in the Zoo" because it was one of my favorites as a child and the story never describes the character beyond having magical spots.

Project requirements were to design a character and create the cover art and one spread.

I based my character off of a flashy bird of paradise with coat tails for his tail feathers. I also chose to put the spots on his coat rather than directly on his feathers. The spots were made by stamping rounds of ink and then arranging them with Photoshop. The coloring is also done in photoshop, though the inking was done by hand.

In the story the spotted character shows off his spot's abilities to two children, here's where he flings them on the clouds.
