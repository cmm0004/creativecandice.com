Aruvio UI Design

For my work with aruvio.com, I was tasked with increasing our organic search visits by enhancing our SEO and providing a better User Interface for the company website. These changes have been incremental and iterative, but after almost a year, some very clear and important changes have occurred.

View Live Site

The Home Page Slider

Among small changes, like making the logo bigger, adding contact information across the top, and re-arranging the contents of the menu, the biggest change to the homepage has been the slider

When I came onboard, the slider was stock potos of business people and a call to submit your information for a demo, but the user wouldn't even have had time to know what Aruvio was selling, why would they submit thier information?

From a usability perspective, the form on the slider had some issues: if you input invalid data (forgot the '@' in your email) then an error message would appear and push the feilds below it off canvas and a user would have to refresh the page in order to submit the form. I offered to change the display of the error messages, but my Marketing Manager wanted a complete overhaul of the slider, completely removing the forms and focusing on our value proposition.
