CEO Council Wireframes

Latest project, redesign of the Atlanta CEO Website

The original site is a wordpress with a blue/green sort of theme, but the owner of the site has asked for a "exclusive, behind-closed-doors" feel. And he doesn't want there to be any pictures, purely typographic. I found a great theme that will work for what he needs, and these wireframes are the layout and flow of content for his site within this new theme so he can make a more informed decision before purchasing it. Made with the Balsamiq Mockup tool.
