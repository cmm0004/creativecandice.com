Document Design

I do print design work for the marketing department, and I've been branding all of our documents that are given out to clients. Our Datasheets, Solutions Briefs, and WhitePapers.

DataSheet Design

View All Datasheets

We have several, but they follow this template. I've blurred out the content intentionally. The information on these is a bit technical, so I chose a long vertical layout that stretches down the entire page to sub-conciously make the reader think of lengthy lines of code.
