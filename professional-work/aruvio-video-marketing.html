Aruvio Videos

I was tasked with creating a series of videos for our marketing and sales departments to showcase our products. I was asked to make them in the 'whiteboard' style. This is my first time using Adobe AfterEffects.

I am full vertical on this project, from ideation, to storyboarding, to art production, to the execution. This is my first time using Adobe AfterEffects. More to come in the future. I expect my execution to become better the more practice I get.

Aruvio Policy Management


Process

I had the idea for a whiteboard video and approched my manager to pitch it. He seemed excited by the idea and I collaborated with sales to get the script (elevator pitch, really) and began sketching out scenes illustrating the script.

The storyboard:

I got approval for the imagery and sequence and started making tight sketches and cleaning them up in photoshop. Animation is done in Adobe AfterEffects.

After the animation was done, I had our intern record herself narrating the script, and matched the audio to the animation.

This will be the first of many, we plan to make a video for each of our major products, I expect the styling and level of finish to evolve as I become more comfortable with the animation process.
