Web Design

The contraints for the webpage was that the layout had to stay the same as the previous page to make implementing it faster for COX's web team, and the Client wanted a realistic gold submit button

This was a good experience in working directly with a client, communicating my design decisions, incorporating what the client wanted, while presenting my work clearly.

After the design work was done, I packaged up all the images and resources I used and sent it to the client.
